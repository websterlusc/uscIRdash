The Administrative Council
The central internal governance structure of the university also includes an Administrative Council which is chaired by the President.
Its membership includes a broad cross-section of internal university leaders and interest groups, namely all Vice Presidents, Deans of Academic Schools,
the Chief Accountant, Librarian, Director of Information Technology, University Registrar, Director of Quality Assurance, Director of Security Health and Safety,
four (4) other directors (on a rotational basis), the University Legal Officer, Associated Student Body President, Faculty Senate President,
Staff Senate President, one (1) faculty member, one (1) staff member,
one (1) Extension Campus Site Coordinator and any other faculty or staff member with specially needed expertise can be asked sit on this council as an invitee.
