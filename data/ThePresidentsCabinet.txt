The President’s Cabinet

The university's day-to-day operations are the responsibility of the President, the university's Chief Executive Officer.
The President is assisted in managing the day-to-day operations of the university by the Provost (the second-ranking officer of the university),
the Vice President for Financial Administration, the Vice President for Administration, Advancement and Planning, the Vice President of Student Services and Enrolment Management,
the Vice President for Spiritual Development and the Associate Provost. The Provost and each Vice-President manage a division of the university as is elaborated later on in this document.
The President, Provost, all Vice Presidents, Associate Provost, and Director of Human Resources form the President’s Cabinet which has executive oversight over the operational activities of the university.
