The Board of Trustees

The supreme decision-making entity for the university is its Board of Trustees which is quinquennially elected. The board comprises of thirty-four persons including the President, Secretary, Treasurer, and Education Director of the CARU, the President of the USC, Presidents of the Conference and Mission fields of the CARU, and at any given time, two (2) Education Directors from the Conference and Mission fields of the CARU on a rotational basis. The board also includes representation from the laity of the Adventist church in the CARU and university alumni. The President of the CARU is the Chairman of the board, the Secretary of the CARU is the Vice-Chairman and the President of the University is the Secretary.
